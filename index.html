Resale Fashion Hub

ResaleFashionHub is a Java-based simulation of an online platform for buying and selling pre-loved fashion items. It promotes sustainability by enabling users to trade gently used clothes, accessories, and shoes.
Functionalities: Admins can add/view products, while users can register/login, add items to cart, checkout, rate products, and view order history.
